About Solo

We were founded with one singular goal in mind: to provide quality lessons in Math & Science at a fraction of the price that you'll find at any of the big chains. This mission of ours got kicked off in December of 2020, when we found out that students at those places were being charged up to $120 per hour!! And we know this wasn't some world famous Nobel-prize-winning tutor giving those lessons - because that tutor works for us now.
