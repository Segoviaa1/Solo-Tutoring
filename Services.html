About Solo

We were founded with one singular goal in mind: to provide quality lessons in Math & Science at a fraction of the price that you'll find at any of the big chains. This mission of ours got kicked off in December of 2020, when we found out that students at those places were being charged up to $120 per hour!! And we know this wasn't some world famous Nobel-prize-winning tutor giving those lessons - because that tutor works for us now. And for basically a third the price, you can get that same service today.





What's Different Here?

Well, there's a bunch of things that the big guys have to pay for that we don't. When you book a session with anyone else, you're funding a whole bunch of stuff that doesn't make your kid any smarter. You're funding rent for the store front, you're funding insurance for if someone get's hurt inside, you're funding paper, toner, and repair costs for when the printer breaks and a healthy dose of corporate greed for the owner.
